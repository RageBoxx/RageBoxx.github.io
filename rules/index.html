Server Rules

  • Respect All Members

    Always treat others with courtesy and professionalism. Discrimination, hate speech, harassment, personal attacks, or any form of disrespect will not be tolerated under any circumstances.

  • Maintain a Safe and Positive Environment

    This server should remain comfortable for everyone. Do not share or discuss content that is explicit, NSFW, violent, excessively graphic, or otherwise inappropriate for public spaces.

  • No Spam or Unapproved Advertising

    Avoid spamming messages, links, or reactions. Advertising, self-promotion, or posting external invites is not permitted unless explicitly approved by the moderation team.

  • Protect Privacy and Personal Information

    Never share personal or sensitive information — your own or anyone else’s — without consent. Doxxing, sharing private messages, or posting identifiable data is strictly prohibited.

  • Follow Channel Guidelines

    Each channel has a specific purpose. Please read and follow the channel descriptions and pinned messages to keep discussions organized and relevant.

  • No Illegal or Unethical Activities

    Do not share, promote, or encourage illegal actions, including piracy, hacking, cheating, scams, or the distribution of copyrighted material without permission.

  • Respect Moderators and Staff

    Moderators and administrators are here to maintain order and fairness. Follow their instructions at all times. If you believe a decision was unfair, you may calmly request clarification in private.

  • Appropriate Usernames, Avatars, and Statuses

    All display names, profile pictures, and custom statuses must remain appropriate and free of offensive, impersonating, or misleading content.

  • Reporting and Conflict Resolution

    If you witness a rule violation or experience an issue, please report it directly to @Staff or use the designated report channel. Do not attempt to handle disputes publicly.

  • Rule Updates and Enforcement

    These rules may be updated at any time to reflect the needs of the community. All members are expected to stay informed by checking the announcements channel. Moderation actions — including warnings, timeouts, mutes, or bans — will be applied based on the severity of the violation.

Our goal is to maintain a welcoming, respectful, and professional community. By staying kind, considerate, and responsible, you help make this server an enjoyable place for everyone. Thank you for being part of the community.
